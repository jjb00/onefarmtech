# OneFarmTech Product Definition

OneFarmTech is a WhatsApp-first farm-to-city procurement platform helping Nigerian restaurants, retailers, caterers, food vendors, large households, and bulk buyers source verified farm produce through managed sourcing, group-buy options, secure payment confirmation, and coordinated delivery.

OneFarmTech is not a typical marketplace. It is a managed procurement platform where the OneFarmTech team controls sourcing, quality checks, order approval, delivery coordination, and buyer relationships.

The platform should protect the current business advantage: trusted farmer relationships, buyer trust, access to produce, and manual operating knowledge.

Farmers should not have login access in the MVP. The OneFarmTech team should manage farmers and suppliers internally.
