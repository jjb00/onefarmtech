# OneFarmTech FAQ Content

## Do I need an account to order?

No. You can start an order directly on WhatsApp. Buyer accounts are useful for repeat customers, businesses, restaurants, hotels, caterers, and people who want order history, invoices, and recurring supply.

## Can I order through WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp is the main ordering channel. You can tell us what you need, confirm price and availability, receive payment details, and get delivery updates through WhatsApp.

## Do farmers sell directly on the platform?

Not at this stage. We work with trusted farmers and rural suppliers, but sourcing is managed by our team to protect quality, fulfilment, and buyer trust.

## Can I split a bulk order with others?

Yes, where group-buy offers are available. For example, if a full bag of potatoes is too much for one buyer, we may open a group-buy so multiple buyers can share the quantity.

## How do group-buys work?

We list selected bulk produce deals with a target quantity, closing date, and delivery or pickup day. Once enough buyers join and pay, the order is sourced and fulfilled.

## When do I pay?

Most individual buyers pay before dispatch. Verified business buyers may qualify for deposit payment, purchase order processing, or short payment terms after approval.

## Can restaurants and hotels order regularly?

Yes. Business buyers can request recurring supply for selected produce on agreed delivery days.

## Can I pay after delivery?

Pay-after-delivery may only be available to approved repeat business buyers with a credit limit. New buyers are usually required to pay before sourcing or dispatch.

## What delivery options are available?

Depending on location and order type, buyers may choose pickup, collection from listed pickup points, platform-arranged delivery, or approved third-party delivery.

## What happens if produce quality is poor?

Quality issues should be reported quickly with photos. We review complaints and may offer replacement, partial credit, or refund depending on the issue and order terms.

## Can I reserve produce before harvest?

For some bulk or business orders, pre-harvest reservation may be available with a deposit.
