# OneFarmTech Payment Rules

## 1. New individual buyers

- 100% payment before sourcing or dispatch.

## 2. New business buyers

- Full payment upfront or deposit before sourcing.
- Admin may approve 70% deposit and 30% balance before delivery.

## 3. Verified business buyers

- May qualify for short credit terms.
- Credit limit must be set by admin.
- Payment terms can be upfront, deposit, 7 days, or 14 days.

## 4. Purchase orders

- Larger restaurants, hotels, caterers, schools, and institutions may submit purchase orders.
- PO does not automatically mean credit is approved.
- Admin must approve PO buyers manually.

## 5. Group-buy orders

- Payment required to reserve a slot.
- If minimum quantity is not reached, admin can refund manually or roll payment to another order.

## 6. Farmer/supplier payment

- Farmers/suppliers should be paid when goods leave them or after quality confirmation at pickup.
- Buyer credit risk should not be passed to farmers.
