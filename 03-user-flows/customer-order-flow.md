# OneFarmTech Customer Order Flow

## Standard WhatsApp Order Flow

1. Customer clicks "Order on WhatsApp" from the website.
2. WhatsApp opens with a prefilled order message.
3. Customer states buyer type, produce needed, quantity, location, and preferred delivery/pickup date.
4. Admin confirms availability, price, delivery method, and payment requirement.
5. Admin creates order in OneFarmTech dashboard.
6. System/admin sends payment instruction or payment link.
7. Customer pays.
8. Admin confirms payment status.
9. Order moves to approved for sourcing.
10. OneFarmTech sources produce from trusted farmer/supplier.
11. Produce is quality checked.
12. Order is packed.
13. Delivery or pickup is arranged.
14. Customer receives order.
15. Order is marked completed.
16. Customer can reorder later.

## Group-Buy Flow

1. Admin creates group-buy offer.
2. Customer sees group-buy on website or receives WhatsApp broadcast.
3. Customer joins by selecting quantity.
4. Customer pays upfront to reserve slot.
5. Quantity is added to group-buy once payment is confirmed.
6. If minimum quantity is reached, group-buy proceeds.
7. If minimum quantity is not reached, admin handles refund or rollover.
8. Order is sourced, checked, packed, and delivered/picked up on scheduled day.

## Business Buyer Flow

1. Restaurant, hotel, caterer, retailer, or food vendor requests recurring supply.
2. Admin creates business buyer profile.
3. Admin records delivery address, preferred produce, order frequency, and payment terms.
4. New business buyers pay upfront or deposit first.
5. Verified business buyers may later receive credit limit or short payment terms.
6. Recurring orders are created manually or from saved templates.
